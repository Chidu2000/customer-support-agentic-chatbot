ACME Corp Warranty Policy (Effective June 2026)

1. Coverage
Most ACME Corp physical products include a 12-month limited warranty from the original purchase date.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects, hardware failures under normal use, and missing parts reported within 14 days of delivery.

2. Extended Coverage
Gold tier customers receive 18 months of warranty coverage.
Platinum tier customers receive 24 months of warranty coverage and priority replacement review.

3. Exclusions
The warranty does not cover:
- Damage from misuse, accidents, unauthorized repairs, or improper installation
- Normal wear and tear, cosmetic scratches, or consumable accessories
- Products without a valid order number or proof of purchase
- Items purchased from unauthorized resellers

4. Resolution Options
After review, ACME Corp may offer troubleshooting, replacement parts, a replacement product, store credit, or a refund when required by applicable policy.
Replacement products may be refurbished, but must meet the same functional standard as the original item.

5. Claim Requirements
Customers must contact support with the order number, product name, issue description, and clear photos or video when relevant.
Support may request basic troubleshooting before approving a warranty claim.

6. Processing Time
Standard warranty claims are reviewed within 3-5 business days.
Gold and Platinum tier claims are reviewed within 1-2 business days.
Approved replacements usually ship within 2 business days after claim approval.
